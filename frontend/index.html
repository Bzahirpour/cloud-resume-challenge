About Ben

I spent over a decade as a master electrician. At 35, I decided I wasn't done growing. No computer science background, no tech degree — just drive. I got my CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ from scratch, cold-messaged data center techs on LinkedIn asking for advice, and landed my first role at ByteDance in May 2024.

Since then: a federal NOC at the FDA, a WAN service provider supporting government and military networks, a B.S. in Network Engineering, and 13+ certifications — all in under two years. I built an entire NOC operations team from nothing: staffing models, SOPs, SolarWinds, ServiceNow, training 17 people with zero prior operations experience.

I'm not the fastest to learn something new. What I have is drive — I study harder, work longer, and care more than most people in this field. That's not a claim. It's a track record.
